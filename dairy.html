What We Offer?

  1. Milk:

    A nutritious drink that comes from cows, goats, or sheep. It's rich in calcium and vitamins.

  2. Flavored Milk:

    This is milk with added flavors like chocolate, strawberry, or vanilla. It's a tasty option for those who enjoy a twist to regular milk.

  3. Cheese:

    A versatile dairy product made by curdling milk. There are many types, from mild cheddar to tangy feta, each with its own flavor profile.

  4. Chocolate:

    While not strictly a dairy product on its own, chocolate often contains milk as an ingredient. It's a popular treat enjoyed in various forms like bars, candies, and hot cocoa.

  5. Ice Cream:

    It's a frozen dessert made from milk, cream, sugar, and flavors like chocolate, vanilla, or fruit. You can enjoy it in cones, cups, or as part of other desserts like sundaes and milkshakes.
